# Client Report Template

This template defines the structure and length constraints for client-facing audit reports.

## Template Selection by Tier

| Tier | Max Lines | Audience | Key Features |
|------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Guest | 400 | DIY Applicants | Actionable guidance, no legal jargon |
| Pro | 500 | RCICs | Technical details, no inline citations |
| Ultra | 600 | Lawyers | Full legal analysis with citations |

---

## GUEST TIER TEMPLATE (Max 400 lines)

```markdown
# AUDIT REPORT

**Application:** [Type]
**Date:** [Date]

---

## VERDICT: [GO | CAUTION | NO-GO]

**Score:** [X/100] - [One sentence rationale]

---

## TOP RISKS

**The Problem:** [1-2 sentences]

**What the Officer Might Ask:** "[Question]"

**Your Defense (Copy & Paste):**
> "[Complete paragraph ready to use in explanation letter]"

**Evidence to Prepare:**
- [Item 1]
- [Item 2]

---

[Repeat for up to 3 risks maximum]

---

## ACTION ITEMS

### Urgent (Within 7-14 Days)
- [ ] [Action with specific instructions]
- [ ] [Action with specific instructions]

### Before Decision
- [ ] [Action]

---

## DISCLAIMER

This report assesses judicial defensibility based on Federal Court precedents. It does NOT predict outcomes or guarantee approval. Immigration officers retain discretion.

**This is not legal advice.** Consult an RCIC or lawyer for representation.

---

**Report Generated:** [Date]
**Tier:** GUEST
```

## Key Principles

### Poison Pill Format (Required for All Tiers)

Every vulnerability MUST include:
1. **The Problem**: 1-2 sentences explaining what officer will flag
2. **Officer's Question**: Exact question in quotes
3. **Defense**: Copy-paste ready paragraph with case reference
4. **Evidence**: Bullet list of supporting documents

### Length Enforcement

| Section | Guest | Pro | Ultra |
|---------|-------|-----|-------|
| Verdict | 5 lines | 8 lines | 10 lines |
| Case Snapshot | 0 | 10 lines | 15 lines |
| Vulnerabilities | 60% | 50% | 45% |
| Strengths | 8 lines | 10 lines | 10 lines |
| Legal Framework | 0 | 0 | 15% |
| Compliance | 10 lines | 15 lines | 15 lines |
| Actions | 15 lines | 20 lines | 25 lines |

### What to NEVER Cut

- Verdict + Score
- Poison Pills (the core value)
- Action Items
- Disclaimer
