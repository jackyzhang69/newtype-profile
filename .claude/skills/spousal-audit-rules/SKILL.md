# Immigration Audit Rules

## Scope
- Hard eligibility checks (fatal blocks)
- Fraud risk badges (soft risk flags)

## Inputs
- Applicant and sponsor profile
- Relationship timeline or study plan

## Outputs
- Eligibility blocks (fatal)
- Risk badges with severity and rationale

## Notes
- Use with `audit_app` to select scenario rules
- Do not change opencode core orchestration
