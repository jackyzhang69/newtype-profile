# Submission Letter Template

Template for client-facing submission letter with work permit application.

**[DATE]**

**[RECIPIENT: Immigration Officer]**
Service Canada / IRCC
[Address]

**RE: Work Permit Application for [Applicant Name]**
**Position**: [Job Title] at [Employer Name]
**IRCC Case Number**: [If available]

---

Dear Immigration Officer,

I am pleased to submit the work permit application for [Applicant Name] for employment as a [Job Title] at [Employer Name], located in [City, Province, Canada].

This letter serves to introduce the application and highlight the key strengths supporting the approval of this work permit.

## 1. INTRODUCTION

[Applicant Name], a [nationality] national with [education level] in [field of study], seeks a work permit to accept employment with [Employer Name] in Canada. The position offers an opportunity for [applicant] to apply [his/her/their] skills and experience in [relevant field], while contributing to [employer]'s operations and growth in Canada.

## 2. EMPLOYMENT OFFER & POSITION

[Employer Name] (Business Number: [BN]) is [brief description of company - size, industry, years in operation]. The company seeks to hire [applicant] in the position of [Job Title], reporting to [reporting structure].

**Position Details**:
- **Title**: [Job Title]
- **Duties**: [Brief description of main responsibilities]
- **Location**: [Address in Canada]
- **Salary**: [Annual salary in CAD]
- **Start Date**: [Date when employment begins]
- **Duration**: [Duration of initial work permit sought]

The position addresses a genuine business need for [company], as [describe labor market need].

## 3. LABOUR MARKET OPINION (LMIA)

[Employer Name] obtained a positive Labour Market Opinion from Service Canada:

- **LMIA File Number**: [Number]
- **Decision**: Positive Assessment
- **Valid Until**: [Expiry date]
- **Position**: [Job title from LMIA]
- **Employer**: [Employer name from LMIA]

The LMIA confirms that:
- [Employer] has met Service Canada's recruitment requirements
- No Canadian workers are available for the position
- The wages offered meet prevailing market rates
- The position is a genuine business need

## 4. APPLICANT QUALIFICATIONS

[Applicant Name] is exceptionally well-qualified for this position:

**Education**:
- **Degree**: [Degree type], [Field of Study]
- **Institution**: [University/College name], [Country]
- **Graduation**: [Year]
- **Relevant Coursework**: [Relevant courses or specializations]

**Professional Experience**:
- [X years] of professional experience in [field]
- Previous employers: [List key positions and employers]
- Demonstrated expertise in: [Key technical/professional skills]
- [Achievement/recognition relevant to new position]

**Languages**:
- [Language 1]: [Fluency level]
- [Language 2]: [Fluency level]

**Certifications**: [Professional certifications relevant to position]

## 5. TIES TO HOME COUNTRY

[Applicant Name] maintains strong ties to [Home Country], ensuring compliance with R200 requirement that applicant intends to leave Canada when the work permit expires:

**Family Ties**:
- [Spouse/Partner] resides in [Home Country]
- [Children] attend school in [Home Country]
- [Aging parents] reside in [Home Country]

**Financial Ties**:
- Owns residential property in [Home Country] (valued at approx. [amount])
- Maintains bank accounts in [Home Country]
- Holds investments in [Home Country]

**Employment Ties**:
- Has been granted leave of absence from current employer [dates]
- Employment with current employer resumes [date when permit expires]
- Professional license maintained in [home country profession]

**No Settlement Indicators**:
- This is [applicant's] first work permit application to Canada
- No family members currently reside in Canada
- No property owned in Canada
- No indication of intent to seek permanent residence

All documents are originals or certified copies, as applicable.

## 7. ADMISSIBILITY CONFIRMATION

[Applicant Name] confirms that:
- [He/She/They] is not inadmissible under IRPA
- [He/She/They] has completed required medical examination with approved result
- [He/She/They] has obtained police certificate from all countries of residence
- [He/She/They] has no criminal record or security concerns
- All information provided in this application is true and accurate

## 8. RETURN PLAN

[Applicant Name] has explicitly committed to returning to [Home Country] at the end of the work permit period. This commitment is evidenced by:

- Spouse and children remaining in [Home Country]
- Substantial property and financial assets in [Home Country]
- Employment position awaiting upon return
- No intention to sponsor family to Canada
- No intention to pursue permanent residence

## 9. COMPLIANCE WITH R200

This application meets all requirements under IRPA R200(1):

- ✓ **Valid Job Offer**: Signed by [Employer] dated [date]
- ✓ **LMIA or Exemption**: Positive Labour Market Opinion obtained
- ✓ **Not Inadmissible**: Medical and police clearances obtained
- ✓ **Temporary Intent**: Strong ties to home country; return plan articulated

## 10. CLOSING

We respectfully request approval of the work permit application for [Applicant Name]. This decision will benefit both [applicant] and Canadian employer [employer], allowing the position to be filled with a qualified professional.

Should you require any additional information or clarification, please do not hesitate to contact [contact person] at [phone/email].

Thank you for your consideration of this application.
