# Standard Disclaimer (SINGLE SOURCE OF TRUTH)

This file is the **canonical source** for the legal disclaimer that appears in all audit reports.

## Disclaimer Text

> This report provides a risk assessment based on historical Federal Court jurisprudence. It is NOT a prediction of future outcomes nor a guarantee of visa issuance. Immigration officers retain broad discretion in their assessment. We assess only the judicial defensibility of the application under current law.

## Usage Rules

1. **Appears ONCE** - At the beginning of the report, after the title/header
2. **NEVER duplicated** - Do NOT add a second disclaimer at the end
3. **NEVER modified** - Use the exact text above
4. **NEVER omitted** - This is a mandatory section in all tiers

## Why This Matters

- **Legal protection**: Manages client expectations
- **Professional standard**: Required by RCIC/lawyer ethics
- **Consistency**: Same disclaimer across all report types and tiers

## App-Specific Skills

App-specific reporter skills (`work-reporter`, `study-reporter`, `spousal-reporter`) do NOT define their own disclaimer. They inherit this disclaimer from `core-reporter`.
