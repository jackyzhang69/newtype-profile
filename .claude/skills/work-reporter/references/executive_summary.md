# Work Permit Executive Summary Template

## Purpose

Generate concise executive summaries for work permit audit reports.

## Template Structure

```markdown
## Executive Summary

**Application Type**: [LMIA/PGWP/ICT/IMP/Open Work Permit/PNP]
**Applicant**: [Name] ([Nationality])
**Position**: [Job Title] at [Employer Name]
**Location**: [City, Province]

### Defensibility Score: [XX]/100

[One-sentence justification based on key findings]

### Overall Assessment: [Excellent/Good/Adequate/Weak/Poor]

### Key Findings

1. **[Finding 1]**: [Brief description]
2. **[Finding 2]**: [Brief description]
3. **[Finding 3]**: [Brief description]
4. **[Finding 4]**: [Brief description] (if applicable)
5. **[Finding 5]**: [Brief description] (if applicable)

### Risk Level Summary

| Category | Risk Level | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| LMIA/Exemption Compliance | [Low/Medium/High/Critical] | [Brief note] |
| Employer Legitimacy | [Low/Medium/High/Critical] | [Brief note] |
| Applicant Credentials | [Low/Medium/High/Critical] | [Brief note] |
| Intent/Ties Assessment | [Low/Medium/High/Critical] | [Brief note] |
| Admissibility | [Low/Medium/High/Critical] | [Brief note] |

### Recommended Next Steps

1. [Priority 1 action]
2. [Priority 2 action]
3. [Priority 3 action]
```

## Category-Specific Variations

### LMIA Applications
- Emphasize LMIA compliance status
- Highlight employer legitimacy findings
- Note wage compliance assessment

### PGWP Applications
- Focus on DLI verification
- Highlight graduation timing
- Note field of study eligibility (post-Nov 2024)

### ICT Applications
- Emphasize corporate relationship verification
- Highlight 12-month employment confirmation
- Note specialized knowledge assessment

### Open Work Permit (Spouse)
- Focus on relationship genuineness
- Highlight principal applicant status
- Note 2025 policy change impact

### PNP Applications
- Emphasize nomination validity
- Highlight job offer consistency
- Note provincial criteria compliance

## Score Interpretation

| Score Range | Assessment | Meaning |
|-------------|------------|---------|
| 85-100 | Excellent | Strong case, minimal risk |
| 70-84 | Good | Solid case, minor concerns |
| 55-69 | Adequate | Viable case, notable risks |
| 40-54 | Weak | Significant concerns |
| 0-39 | Poor | Major issues, high refusal risk |

## Constraints

- Executive summary MUST be under 300 words
- Use bullet points for key findings
- Include defensibility score with justification
- Avoid legal guarantees or outcome predictions
