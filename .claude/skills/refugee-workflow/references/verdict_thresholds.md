# Refugee Protection Verdict Thresholds

## Verdict Mapping

| Score Range | Risk Level | Verdict | Action |
|-------------|------------|---------|--------|
| 80-100 | Low | **GO** | Proceed with confidence to hearing |
| 60-79 | Medium | **CAUTION** | Proceed with strengthened evidence |
| 40-59 | High | **CAUTION** or **NO-GO** | Significant preparation needed |
| 0-39 | Critical | **NO-GO** | Fundamental issues present |

## Verdict Definitions

### GO (80-100)
- Strong credibility indicators
- Clear persecution nexus to Convention ground
- Adequate identity documentation
- No IFA or state protection concerns
- **Action**: Proceed to RPD hearing with standard preparation

### CAUTION (60-79)
- Some credibility gaps but addressable
- Persecution nexus established but needs strengthening
- Identity documentation adequate
- Minor IFA/state protection concerns
- **Action**: Address specific gaps, prepare explanation for weak points

### CAUTION/NO-GO Boundary (40-59)
- Significant credibility concerns
- Weak persecution nexus
- Identity documentation gaps
- IFA or state protection likely arguments
- **Decision Criteria**: Upgrade to CAUTION if country conditions evidence can overcome IFA concerns

### NO-GO (0-39)
- Fundamental credibility issues
- No persecution nexus to Convention ground
- Identity not established
- Clear IFA or state protection available
- **Action**: Do not proceed; consider PRRA or other options

## Poison Pill Rules (A101 Ineligibility)

These conditions automatically trigger NO-GO regardless of score:

1. **A101(1)(a)** - Convention refugee status in another country
2. **A101(1)(b)** - Prior rejected claim under A108-109
3. **A101(1)(c)** - Previous claim abandoned/withdrawn
4. **A101(1)(c.1)** - Declared abandoned claim not reopened
5. **A101(1)(d)** - Prior claim rejected by PRRA
6. **A101(1)(e)** - STCA bar (Safe Third Country Agreement)
7. **A101(1)(f)** - Ineligible under security/human rights grounds

## Article 1F Exclusion Grounds

These conditions trigger automatic NO-GO:

| Ground | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **1F(a)** | Crimes against peace, war crimes, crimes against humanity |
| **1F(b)** | Serious non-political crime outside Canada |
| **1F(c)** | Acts contrary to UN purposes and principles |

## STCA Exception Analysis

If STCA bar applies, check exceptions:

| Exception | Criteria | Score Modifier |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Family member in Canada | Canadian citizen/PR/protected person/valid permit holder | Remove STCA Poison Pill |
| Unaccompanied minor | Under 18, no parent/guardian in Canada or US | Remove STCA Poison Pill |
| Document holder | Valid Canadian visa, work permit, study permit | Remove STCA Poison Pill |
| Charges/convictions | Death penalty or certain offenses in US | Remove STCA Poison Pill |

**If exception met**: Remove STCA Poison Pill, apply normal scoring

## Cessation Risk Assessment

| Indicator | Score Impact |
|-----------|--------------|
| Returned to country of persecution | -20 |
| Renewed passport from country of persecution | -15 |
| Re-established in country of persecution | -30 (likely Poison Pill) |
| Changed circumstances (objective) | Assess on merits |
