## Document Preparation Notes

### Translation Requirements

- All non-English/French documents need certified translation
- Translator must certify accuracy
- Keep original with translation

### Certification

- Some documents may need apostille or authentication
- Check with representative about requirements

### Organization

- Number all documents
- Create index/table of contents
- Reference documents in BOC
- Keep copies of everything submitted
