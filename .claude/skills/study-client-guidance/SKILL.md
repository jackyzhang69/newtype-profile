# Study Permit Client Guidance

## Scope
- Study plan writing guide
- Document preparation checklist (IMM 5483/5555)
- Interview preparation tips
- Common mistakes to avoid

## Outputs
- Client-facing guidance documents
- Preparation checklists
- Sample templates

## Key Documents

### 1. Study Plan Guide
- Structure and content recommendations
- What to include and avoid
- Sample outlines

### 2. Document Checklist
Based on IMM 5483 (outside Canada) and IMM 5555 (inside Canada):
- Identity documents
- Admission documents
- Financial documents
- Educational documents
- Supporting documents

### 3. Interview Preparation
- Common officer questions
- Response strategies
- Do's and don'ts

## 2024-2025 Policy Updates

### PAL Requirement
- Required since January 22, 2024
- Exemptions: Master/PhD, K-12, exchange students

### Financial Threshold
- $20,635/year living cost (since January 1, 2024)
- GIC amount: $20,635 for SDS

### SDS Changes
- Processing: ~20 business days
- IELTS 6.0+ or equivalent required
- Pre-paid tuition required
