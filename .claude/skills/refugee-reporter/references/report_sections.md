# Report Sections Reference

## Full Report Structure

A complete refugee audit report contains these sections in order:

1. Executive Summary
2. Applicant Profile
3. Claim Summary
4. Eligibility Analysis
5. Risk Analysis
6. Legal Research
7. Evidence Assessment
8. Recommendations
9. Disclaimer

## Disclaimer (Required at End)

```markdown
---

## Disclaimer

This report provides a risk assessment based on historical Federal Court jurisprudence and Immigration and Refugee Board decision patterns. It does NOT predict the outcome of any specific refugee claim or guarantee success before the IRB. IRB members retain full discretion in their determinations based on the evidence and testimony presented at hearing.

This assessment evaluates judicial defensibility and identifies potential risk areas. It should not replace professional legal advice from a qualified immigration lawyer or consultant. Claimants should consult with qualified legal counsel regarding their specific circumstances.

The information in this report is current as of the audit date noted above and may not reflect subsequent changes in law, policy, or country conditions.

---
Report generated by Immigration Audit OS
```
