# Study Permit Verdict Thresholds

## Verdict Mapping

| Score Range | Risk Level | Verdict | Action |
|-------------|------------|---------|--------|
| 80-100 | Low | **GO** | Proceed with confidence |
| 60-79 | Medium | **CAUTION** | Proceed with mitigations |
| 40-59 | High | **CAUTION** or **NO-GO** | Conditional proceed or reject |
| 0-39 | Critical | **NO-GO** | Do not proceed as-is |

## Verdict Definitions

### GO (80-100)
- Clear genuine study intent demonstrated
- Strong financial capacity proven
- Solid ties to home country
- No dual intent concerns
- **Action**: Submit application with standard preparation

### CAUTION (60-79)
- Study intent reasonable but needs strengthening
- Financial proof adequate but borderline
- Some ties concerns addressable
- Minor dual intent indicators
- **Action**: Address specific gaps before submission

### CAUTION/NO-GO Boundary (40-59)
- Weak study plan rationale
- Financial capacity questionable
- Limited ties to home country
- Significant dual intent concerns
- **Decision Criteria**: Upgrade to CAUTION if study plan can be strengthened with concrete career path

### NO-GO (0-39)
- No genuine study intent evident
- Insufficient funds with no reasonable source
- Strong immigration intent indicators
- Previous refusals for same issues
- **Action**: Do not submit; major reassessment needed

## Poison Pill Rules

These conditions automatically trigger NO-GO regardless of score:

1. **R215(1) Ineligibility** - Does not meet study permit requirements
2. **PAL Requirement Not Met** - No valid Provincial Attestation Letter (post Jan 22, 2024)
3. **Financial Fraud Indicators** - Fake bank statements, borrowed funds
4. **Misrepresentation History** - Previous A40 finding
5. **Multiple Prior Refusals** - 2+ refusals for same program type without significant changes

## Study-Specific Thresholds

### Academic Progression Logic

| Transition | Score Modifier |
|------------|----------------|
| Bachelor → Master (same field) | +5 |
| Bachelor → Master (related field) | 0 |
| Bachelor → Master (unrelated field) | -10 |
| Master → Second Master | -15 (needs strong justification) |
| Work experience → Study | 0 (if career advancement clear) |
| Study gap > 5 years | -10 |

### Financial Adequacy

| Funds vs. Requirement | Score Impact |
|----------------------|--------------|
| ≥ 150% | +10 to Evidence dimension |
| 100-149% | 0 |
| 80-99% | -10 |
| < 80% | Poison Pill (NO-GO) |

## Score Override Conditions

| Condition | Override |
|-----------|----------|
| Study plan score ≥ 80 AND Financial ≥ 70 | Minimum score: 65 |
| Any dimension < 40 | Maximum score: 65 |
| Any Poison Pill present | Force NO-GO |
| All Poison Pills cleared | Allow score-based verdict |
