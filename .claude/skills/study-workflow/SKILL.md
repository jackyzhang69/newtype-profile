# Study Permit Workflow Templates

## Scope
- Primary assessment template
- Deep analysis template
- Final review template
- Internal workflow guidance

## Workflow Stages

### Stage 1: Primary Assessment
- Quick eligibility check
- Document inventory
- Risk flag identification
- Initial scoring

### Stage 2: Deep Analysis
- Detailed risk evaluation
- Evidence strength assessment
- Case law research integration
- Mitigation strategy development

### Stage 3: Final Review
- Quality control check
- Citation verification
- Consistency validation
- Risk summary compilation

## Outputs
- Stage-specific assessment reports
- Risk matrices with scores
- Mitigation recommendations

## Key Assessment Areas

| Area | Weight | Key Questions |
|------|--------|---------------|
| Study Plan | 30% | Is it logical, specific, realistic? |
| Financial | 25% | Sufficient? Source clear? |
| Ties to Home | 25% | Strong enough to ensure return? |
| Background | 10% | Education/work progression logical? |
| Immigration History | 10% | Any red flags? |
