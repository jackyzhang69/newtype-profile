# Spousal Sponsorship Verdict Thresholds

## Verdict Mapping

| Score Range | Risk Level | Verdict | Action |
|-------------|------------|---------|--------|
| 80-100 | Low | **GO** | Proceed with confidence |
| 60-79 | Medium | **CAUTION** | Proceed with mitigations |
| 40-59 | High | **CAUTION** or **NO-GO** | Conditional proceed or reject |
| 0-39 | Critical | **NO-GO** | Do not proceed as-is |

## Verdict Definitions

### GO (80-100)
- Strong evidence of genuine relationship
- No credibility concerns
- Complete documentation
- No admissibility issues
- **Action**: Submit application with standard preparation

### CAUTION (60-79)
- Some evidence gaps but addressable
- Minor credibility concerns
- Mostly complete documentation
- No fatal admissibility issues
- **Action**: Address specific gaps before submission

### CAUTION/NO-GO Boundary (40-59)
- Significant evidence gaps
- Moderate credibility concerns
- Critical documentation missing
- Potential admissibility concerns
- **Decision Criteria**: Upgrade to CAUTION if all gaps are addressable within 30 days

### NO-GO (0-39)
- Fundamental relationship genuineness concerns
- Serious credibility issues (contradictions, fraud indicators)
- Critical documentation impossible to obtain
- Hard admissibility bars (sponsorship bar, previous refusal)
- **Action**: Do not submit; reassess after major changes

## Poison Pill Rules

These conditions automatically trigger NO-GO regardless of score:

1. **Sponsorship Undertaking Bar** - Sponsor has existing undertaking not yet fulfilled
2. **Marriage of Convenience Indicators** - Multiple red flags for non-genuine relationship
3. **Active Removal Order** - Applicant under removal order
4. **Misrepresentation Finding** - Previous A40 finding within 5 years
5. **Criminal Inadmissibility** - Serious criminality without rehabilitation

## Score Override Conditions

| Condition | Override |
|-----------|----------|
| All 4 dimensions >= 70 | Minimum score: 65 |
| Any dimension < 40 | Maximum score: 65 |
| Any Poison Pill present | Force NO-GO |
| All Poison Pills cleared | Allow score-based verdict |
